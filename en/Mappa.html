Where you can find us

The Student Theater is located in Via di Sardegna 420 in the district Sallustiano, located in the historic center of the city of Rome. Embraced by the streets of Corso d'Italia and Vittorio Veneto, the theater is located in the richest area of history and culture of the capital.
