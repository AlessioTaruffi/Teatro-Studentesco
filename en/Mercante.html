Il Mercante di Venezia

Stagione 2022/23

Description

The Venetian noble Bassanio, having squandered his possessions, asks the merchant Antonio for a loan in order to court the heiress Porzia. Antonio, waiting for the return of his ships, borrows the money from the Jewish usurer Shylock who makes the loan, on the condition that if the money is not repaid on the agreed day, he will have the right to withdraw a pound of flesh from the body of Anthony. Porzia will marry Bassanio but, since Antonio's ships are wrecked, the debt is not paid; Shylock claims the pound of flesh which, according to the judge's ironic order, he will be able to remove, but without bloodshed. Unable to do so, the usurer gives up and loses his wealth, which he will get back only if he converts to Christianity and leaves it, after his death, to his daughter Gessica, who has fled to marry a Christian. The complex character of Shylock, isolated and aware of his diversity and marginalization, has often been played by actors who, exaggerating his tragedy, have made him (like E. Kean) the hero of a persecuted race.
