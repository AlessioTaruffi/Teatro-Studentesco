The Bethrothed

Season 2022/23

Duration: 120 minutes

From: €12,00

Description

Characters and Facts take place against the background of the Lombard countryside and the city of Milan, between 1628 and 1630. Don Abbondio is the curate in charge of celebrating the wedding of two young spinners, Renzo and Lucia. The henchmen of Don Rodrigo, the lord of the town, order him not to celebrate the wedding. The priest is unable to resist the threats and invents excuses to postpone the wedding, eventually confessing that the one who opposes it is Don Rodrigo. Renzo and Lucia, together with her mother, Agnese, and father Cristoforo, their spiritual guide, try to remedy the abuse. Renzo goes to the lawyer Azzeccagarbugli, while Father Cristoforo goes to Don Rodrigo's castle to try to convince him to change his mind. Both will be badly chased away. The young people just have to try a surprise wedding. The so-called 'night of cheating' arrives: the wedding of the two young spinners fails, but also the attempted kidnapping of Lucia concocted by Don Rodrigo. Meanwhile, Father Cristoforo has organized the escape of the two young men who will take Renzo to Milan and Lucia together with Agnese to the convent in Monza, under the protection of Gertrude. Renzo will arrive in the city in a period shaken by popular revolts; on this occasion the young man will make an invective against the powerful which will lead him to be sought after by the cops as leader of the rioters. Don Rodrigo still attempts the kidnapping of Lucia relying on an obscure lord of the time, the Unnamed, who entrusts the first of her good, the Nibbio, with the connivance of the nun of Monza and Egidio, her lover. Lucia is kidnapped and taken to the castle of the Unnamed: there she takes a vow of chastity thus pitying her kidnapper. Repentant, the Unnamed lets Lucia go and she finds herself involved in the "Milanese disasters": the descent of the lansquenets and the plague epidemic. Lucia, Father Cristoforo and Don Rodrigo will also be infected by the plague and taken to the hospital where, at the end of various vicissitudes, Renzo will finally arrive. The latter forgives the dying Don Rodrigo, while Father Cristoforo fulfills Lucia's vow of chastity. After the plague, the two young people can finally find each other and begin a prosperous future together.
