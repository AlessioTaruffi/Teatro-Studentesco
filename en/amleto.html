Hamlet

Descrizione

In Elsinore, Denmark, the ghost of the late king appears ad Hamlet, his son, and asks him to be avenged: in fact, it was his brother Claudio who killed him, who is now seized the throne and married the king's widowed wife, Gertrude. The prince assures that he will keep his promise but, eager to have concrete proof of his guilt uncle, he decides to delay the moment of revenge. The certainty that the uncle is responsible for the death of the father it comes to him when, during a theatrical show, he stages the murder of the late king and Claudius, indignant and at the same time frightened, he gets up and leaves. Going to talk to his mother to justify his behavior, Hamlet kills Polonius by mistake, adviser to the new king and father of Ophelia and Laertes. The latter, who returned to Denmark as soon as he heard the news, declared Hamlet guilty of his father's death and of his sister - who in the meantime has decided to kill herself to put an end to her pain - and challenges him to a duel. In agreement with Claudius, Laertes sprinkles the tip of his sword with a lethal substance and also poisons his cup where Hamlet will have to drink in case of victory. In a series of random events, however, it is the mother Gertrude who is drinking from the poisoned cup and lethal sword strikes both duelists; before dying, however, Hamlet manages to kill Claudius.
