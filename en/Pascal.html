Il Fu Mattia Pascal

Season 2022/23

Duration: 100 minuti

From: €10,00

Description

Mattia Pascal, believed dead in his country, tries to live a new life under another name, in Rome. But soon a series of painful adventures and a sense of emptiness persuade him of the impossibility of escaping from that "form" that society has given him; and he tries to get back into it. Too late: his wife has remarried and life goes on without him, who is therefore dead despite being alive.
